About Us

FRIAS is a well-known company that excel in fashon business. Our company was first founded by Mr. Harry Chan in March 2014, we have created a lot of product related to fashion especially for kids. Our product is recognized by many top famous designer around the world such as, Ralph Lauren, Christian Dior, and Marc Jacobs.
Our mission is simply to provide the highest quality, and the most comfortable clothes for kids of all ages. We also provide a new special service that can’t be found in any other stores around Singapore, which is a full body measurements directly to your home so it will allow kids to have the best fit of their chosen fashion wear.
