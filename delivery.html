Delivery Service

FRIAS now can deliver your purchase item directly into your desired place. After you have successfully placed your order, our Customer Service team from FRIAS will contact you for confirmation as soon as possible by Email, SMS, or Phone.
Notice:
After successful verification, you can expect to receive your order within the following time frame:

  • For Local areas:
  • Delivery via FRIAS delivery
  • No charge added if your order is more than $50sgd
  • Trusted delivery within 2-4 working days (if your delivery didn’t arrive within the arranged date, you can inform our customer support for the delivery detail and you can get 50% discount voucher on selected items)
  • For International shipping:
  • Shipping via DHL or FedEx
  • For the cost of international shipping, we will send you an email regarding the shipping detail)

If you have any issue regarding the delivery, you can contact our Customer Service team to get a more specific delivery details.
