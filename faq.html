Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What if my package doesn’t arrive on time?
A: If the package doesn’t arrive on time we will give you compensation, mostly will be a 50% discount voucher on our selected items.

Q: Can I exchange my items if there’s some deficiency?
A: Of course we will exchange your item for free. This will apply only if it is not more than 7 days after the day you received the package. If it’s more than 7 days, we will not accept any exchange.

Q: Do you sell a fashion product for adults?
A: FRIAS now only sell fashion product only for kids, but in the near future we might sell other products for adults.

Q:  Do you have any store in Singapore?
A: We will have a FRIAS café in 8th October 2015, which we will also sell all our products there.

Q: Is there a minimal order for each purchase?
A: We don’t have a minimal or maximum order for each purchase, so it’s up to you whether you only want to buy 1 product or more than 1.
