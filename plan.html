Mission Statement

The mission of the website is to bring out the excitement of todays kids with with exciting fashion lifestyle products. The site is also needed to show and help the customer about the current product, updates, events, services, and many more. It will be very benefical for our client, because it could help the client to choose and order via online without the need for the client to go the the offical store.

Success Evaluation

The site can be said succesfull if there are a lot of customer order the product via online, which could help FRIAS to become more well-known and increase their sales. And if there are many visitors that come to see our product via online.

Target Audience

1.The target audience of this website is mostly parents and their kids.

2.We will design this website with an refreshing and childish design yet it's simple to use by anyone.
