FRIAS ORDER and RETURN POLICY

FRIAS believes customer satisfaction is main priority, and has therefore a liberal refund policy. Under this policy:

1. Your order will be processed maximum 48 hours. If you haven’t received an email or call regarding the order, please contact our customer service immediately.

2. Your order will be processed if the payment is made within 48 hours after placing an order.

3. Refunds will be considered only if the request is made within 48 hours of placing an order.

4. FRIAS return can be made if the customer establishes that the quality of product delivered is not good.

5. If you feel that the product you received is not as shown on the site or as your expectations, you must call or email our FRIAS customer service within 24 hours of receiving the product. FRIAS customer Service Team will look after your complaint and take the appropriate decision.

6. When you buy our products/services, your purchase is covered by our 21-day money-back guarantee.
